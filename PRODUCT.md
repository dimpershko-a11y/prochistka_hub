# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PRO-CHISTKA Hub is used by the owner of a cleaning company, office managers who coordinate orders and clients, and employees working on-site. They use it in a practical operational context: checking current work, calculating cleaning jobs, preparing documents, tracking people, money, clients, partners, and order status without jumping between separate tools.

## Product Purpose

The product exists to become one modular web app for the core business processes of PRO-CHISTKA. Success means the hub feels trustworthy enough to run daily operations from one place while keeping each business module independent: calculator, dashboard, checklists, timesheet, damages, documents, CRM, orders, expenses, employees, partners, and settings.

## Brand Personality

The voice is professional, confident, clean, and technological. The interface should feel like a serious working dashboard for a service business: precise, calm, premium, and efficient without turning into a decorative landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid cheap template landing pages, overloaded cards, random gradients, chaotic icon styles, acidic colors, and visual noise that distracts from operational decisions. The product should not look like a generic AI-generated SaaS shell or a marketing page wearing dashboard furniture.

## Design Principles

- Put operations first: every screen should make the next business action clear.
- Earn trust through clarity: names, numbers, statuses, and controls should be easy to scan and hard to misread.
- Keep the system modular: each module can have its own workflow, but navigation, component behavior, and shared states should stay consistent across the hub.
- Use premium restraint: visual polish should come from spacing, hierarchy, typography, and disciplined color, not decoration.
- Design for real field conditions: managers and employees may use the hub quickly, under time pressure, and on different screen sizes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve readable typography, visible focus states, keyboard access for core navigation, touch-friendly controls for on-site use, and reduced-motion alternatives for any animation.
